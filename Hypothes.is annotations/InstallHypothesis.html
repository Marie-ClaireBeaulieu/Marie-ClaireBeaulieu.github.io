Install hypothes.is ### Chrome 1. Go to [http://hypothes.is](http://hypothes.is) 2. Click **Install Chrome Extension** ### Firefox 1. Go to [http://hypothes.is](http://hypothes.is) 2. From the application menu go to View > Toolbars > Bookmarks Toolbar 3. Drag and drop the **Launch Hypothesis** button onto the Bookmarks Toolbar ### Open hypothes.is If hypothes.is doesn't open automatically, open it manually. In Chrome click its icon. ![hypothes.is Chrome launch](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PerseusDL/perseids_docs/master/joth/screenshots/chrome_icon.png) In Firefox click **Launch Hypothesis** from your bookmarks toolbar. ![hypothes.is Firefox launch](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PerseusDL/perseids_docs/master/joth/screenshots/firefox_launch.png) You know hypothes.is is open if you see a comic text bubble and a red eye icon on the right side of your browser window.
