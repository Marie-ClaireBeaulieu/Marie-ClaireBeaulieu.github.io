The project

You'll be annotating passages in "A Dictionary of Greek and Roman Biography and Mythology" by William Smith.

The questions we're interested in answering are:

  • Who is this character?
  • What is this character's relationship with other characters in the text.
  • How did the ancients describe (characterize) this figure?
  • What does this tell us about the figure? Can we gain information from the network that would not be apparent otherwise?
