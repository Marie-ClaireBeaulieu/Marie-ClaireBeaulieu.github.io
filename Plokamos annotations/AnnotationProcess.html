Prerequisites
What you need to do before getting started

  1. Navigate to Smith's Dictionary using this link
  2. Click on "Smith" then "English"
  3. Find the entry you wish to annotate in the alphabetical list. This should be a figure present on the object you are analyzing for your paper or a related figure you wish to learn more about Be careful, many figures have the same name in Greek mythology! Read the entry before starting your work!
  5. Select the word you want to annotate and click the gear icon on the pop-up
  6. Enter the values in the form using the pre-populated matches and click "apply"
  7. View your annotation as a pop-up on hover and as a graph
  8. When you are finished, commit by clicking the cloud icon on the bottom left
  9. On the appointed date, submit your ID number on the Trunk assignment list. Be sure to include your commentary as a Microsoft Word document
  10. Once you have received instructor feedback, integrate what you have learned from your annotation work to your term project
