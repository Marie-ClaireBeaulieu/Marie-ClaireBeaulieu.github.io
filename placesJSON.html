Instructions for Place Annotations Using JSON-LD

In this part of the project, we must indicate the places with which a mythological figure is associated. Find place names in your Smith entry and follow the steps below. You must create one annotation per unique place name mentioned in the entry in relation to your figure.

Step 1

Copy and paste the following in a plain text document:
